Meet the Editor

Welcome to Thrust & Theory. I'm Vick Maturu, the founder and editor of this project! I have always had a keen interest in aerospace and space exploration since I was a kid, and I’m slowly turning my childhood dreams into reality. I'm a Systems Engineer with a double degree in Computer Science and Data Science. In my free time, I work on launching model rockets from simple items, and I love designing and developing new machinery.

Our Journey

2023: The idea for Thrust & Theory is born during a late-night conversation about SpaceX, Saturn V, and rocket reusability.

Early 2024: First sketches and wireframes designed; major research into aerospace history and propulsion systems begins.

Mid 2024: Development of the initial website starts using HTML, CSS, and creative tools like Photoshop and Illustrator.

Spring 2025: Thrust & Theory officially launches, blending rocketry passion with data-driven storytelling and exploration!
